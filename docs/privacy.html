Runtime Viewer Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-07

Runtime Viewer is a developer tool for inspecting Objective-C and Swift runtime interfaces of Mach-O binaries on the local device. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle.

Information We Collect

Runtime Viewer does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Specifically, the app does not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information.
  • Use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • Send any data to servers operated by the developer.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Application preferences (theme, layout, source switcher state).
  • User-created bookmarks of runtime classes, protocols, and members.
  • Cached results of binary parsing for faster reopen.

This data never leaves your device.

Local Network Access

Runtime Viewer can optionally discover other instances of itself on the same local network via Bonjour (_runtimeviewer._tcp) to mirror runtime information between your own devices. The app only communicates with peers you explicitly choose to connect to, over the local network. No data is sent to the public internet.

Children's Privacy

Runtime Viewer is intended for developers and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
